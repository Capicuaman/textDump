### Bilan Overview
- **Company**: Bilan
- **Type**: Electolyte salt production and sales company.
- **Main Product**: An electrolyte mix formulated to replace sodium, potassium, and magnesium lost through sweat. 
  - **Dosage**: 3g to be dissolved in 300ml of water.
  - **Slogan**: "Clean functional hydration" emphasizes a natural, additive-free formula.

### Marketing and Sales
- **Sales Strategy**: Direct-to-consumer (DTC) via website subscriptions, wholesale to gyms and clinics, B2B partnerships with sports teams or corporate programs.
- **Target Audience**: 
  - Primary: Athletes and fitness enthusiasts.
  - Secondary: Corporate wellness programs and medical clinics.
