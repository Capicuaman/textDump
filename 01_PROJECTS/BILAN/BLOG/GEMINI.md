# GEMINI.md - Directory Context

---

## Directory Overview

This directory serves as a content creation hub for a project focused on **electrolytes and hydration**. It is a non-code project containing synthesized research and a strategic content plan for developing blog posts or other informational materials.

## Key Files

1.  **`bestElectrolyte.md`**
    *   **Purpose:** This is the core research document. It contains a comprehensive summary of findings on electrolytes and hydration, synthesized from reputable medical and health sources (Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, Harvard, etc.).
    *   **Content:** The file defines what electrolytes are, explains their function, details their connection to hydration, discusses natural sources, and outlines the dangers of imbalance.

2.  **`electrolyteBlog.md`**
    *   **Purpose:** This file is a strategic content plan for a blog.
    *   **Content:** It outlines a five-part blog series titled "The Power of Balance: Your Ultimate Guide to Electrolytes and Hydration." It details the theme, target audience, and key talking points for each planned post, covering topics from introductory concepts to practical dietary tips and health warnings.

## Usage

The contents of this directory are intended for content development.

*   Use **`bestElectrolyte.md`** as the primary source of factual information and for fact-checking.
*   Use **`electrolyteBlog.md`** to guide the creation of individual articles or content pieces, following the outlined themes and points.
