# BILAN Blog Content Strategy: 25 High-Conversion Posts
**Strategic Content Roadmap Ranked by Conversion Potential**

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines 25 blog post concepts ranked by conversion potential, designed specifically for BILAN's target audience (athletes, fitness enthusiasts, yoga practitioners, health-conscious individuals). Each post is evaluated on:

- **Product Education (20%)** - How well it educates about electrolytes/BILAN's unique value
- **Problem-Solution Fit (25%)** - Addresses real pain points that drive purchase
- **Audience Match (20%)** - Resonates with BILAN's core customer segments
- **SEO/Traffic Potential (15%)** - Likelihood to rank and drive organic search
- **Call-to-Action Opportunity (20%)** - Natural place to recommend BILAN

## Conversion Optimization Notes

1. **All posts should include:**
   - A specific CTA (BILAN packet mention)

   - FAQ-style section with common questions

2. **Track metrics for each post:**
   - Time on page
   - Bounce rate
   - Links to product page
   - Conversion rate
   - Return visitor percentage

3. **A/B test CTA language:**
