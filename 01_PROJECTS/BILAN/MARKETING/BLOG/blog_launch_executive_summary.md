## FAQ: Common Questions

**Q: Do I need all 25 posts to make this work?**
A: No. Posts 1-8 (Tier 1) will give you 80% of the results. Posts 17-25 are long-tail, niche-specific, but helpful for 6+ month ROI.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: First visible results: 4-6 weeks. Meaningful traffic: 2-3 months. Significant revenue: 3-6 months. This assumes good execution and backlink strategy.

**Q: Can I do this without hiring writers/designers?**
A: Yes. Use Canva (design) + ChatGPT/Claude (first drafts) + your editing. Takes more time but costs $13-30/month. Tradeoff: speed vs. cost.

**Q: Which posts should I prioritize?**
A: Posts 1, 3, 6 first (highest traffic potential). Then Posts 2, 4, 5, 7, 8 (authority building). Posts 9-25 are supporting content.

**Q: How do I know if this is working?**
A: Week 4-8: Rank check (are any posts in top 20?). Week 8-12: Traffic check (500+ visitors/month?). Month 3-4: Email signups (300+ signups/month?). Month 4-6: Sales (revenue from email list?).

**Q: What if my traffic is lower than projected?**
A: Common reasons: (1) Backlink strategy missing (critical!), (2) Poor keyword targeting, (3) Title/meta description issues, (4) Content quality (too thin). Fix by: Adding backlinks, optimizing SEO, expanding content depth, improving writing.

**Q: Can I launch fewer posts and expand later?**
A: Absolutely. Launch Posts 1, 3, 6 first. Measure performance. Launch Posts 2, 4, 5, 7, 8 next month. This lets you learn and optimize as you go.
