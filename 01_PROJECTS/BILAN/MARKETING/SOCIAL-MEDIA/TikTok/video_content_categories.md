## Category Selection Framework

### When to Use Each Category:

**Use EDUCATIVO when:**
- Launching new product information
- Responding to common misconceptions
- Building long-term authority
- Creating saveable reference content

**Use ROMPE-MITOS when:**
- Competitors make claims you can counter
- Industry has widespread misinformation
- You want to create debate/discussion
- Differentiating from competitors

**Use ANTES/DESPU√âS when:**
- You have strong customer testimonials
- Building social proof is priority
- Conversion is the goal
- Creating aspirational content

**Use TIPS R√ÅPIDOS when:**
- Need high completion rate content
- Testing new audience segments
- Want quick wins for algorithm
- Providing immediate value

**Use TENDENCIAS when:**
- There's a relevant trending sound/format
- Want to reach new audience via FYP
- Algorithm needs a boost
- Testing creative approaches

**Use PRODUCT SHOWCASE when:**
- New product launch or feature
- E-commerce campaign active
- Need to educate on usage
- Building product desire

**Use COMPARISONS when:**
- Prospects are evaluating alternatives
- Competitor makes misleading claims
- Price objection needs addressing
- Conversion-focused campaign

**Use LIFESTYLE/BTS when:**
- Building brand personality
- Founder wants to be face of brand
- Transparency is selling point
- Creating emotional connection

**Use COMMUNITY/UGC when:**
- You have strong customer content
- Building loyalty is priority
- Social proof is needed
- Engagement is low (reactivate community)

**Use ENTERTAINMENT when:**
- Need viral boost
- Reaching younger demographic
- Testing humor and personality
- Share rate is priority
