## HOW TO USE THESE PROMPTS

### **General Guidelines:**
- Copy the full prompt text for your AI image generator
- Add platform-specific parameters at the end (e.g., `--ar 16:9 --v 6` for Midjourney)
- Generate 4-6 variations of each prompt
- Select the best 1-2 for product compositing
- Use Photoshop/Canva to add your actual BILAN product bag over the generated background

### **Product Integration:**
These prompts generate backgrounds/scenes. You'll need to:
1. Generate the background scene
2. Photograph your BILAN product separately (or use existing product photos)
3. Composite the product onto the generated background using photo editing software
4. Ensure lighting and perspective match for realistic results

**Alternative:** Some prompts include the product. Use these if your AI tool can consistently generate product packaging (less reliable).

## POST-GENERATION WORKFLOW

### **Step 1: Generate Multiple Variations**
- Generate 4-6 versions of each prompt
- Select 1-2 best backgrounds
- Save at highest resolution available

### **Step 2: Product Photography**
- Photograph your BILAN bag separately with similar lighting
- Use white/neutral background for easy extraction
- Match the lighting direction of generated background

### **Step 3: Compositing**
Use Photoshop, Canva Pro, or Pixlr:
1. Remove background from product photo
2. Place product onto generated background
3. Match lighting and shadows
4. Adjust color balance to blend naturally
5. Add realistic shadows if needed
6. Apply subtle blur to product edges for realism

### **Step 4: Final Touches**
- Adjust overall color grading for consistency
- Ensure BILAN logo is sharp and readable
- Add subtle vignette if needed
- Export in appropriate resolution:
  - Social media: 1080x1350px (4:5) or 1080x1920px (9:16)
  - Website: 2400x1350px (16:9)
  - Print: 300dpi at final size

## TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON AI ISSUES

### **Issue: Product Packaging Doesn't Look Right**
**Solution:** Use "Background Only" prompts and composite real product photo

### **Issue: Lighting Looks Artificial**
**Solution:** Add terms like "natural window light," "golden hour," "soft diffused lighting"

### **Issue: Too Much Clutter**
**Solution:** Specify "minimalist," "clean composition," "negative space"

### **Issue: Unrealistic Shadows**
**Solution:** Add "realistic shadows," "natural shadow fall," "soft shadow"

### **Issue: Wrong Colors**
**Solution:** Specify exact colors: "white marble," "sage green," "turquoise blue"

### **Issue: Blurry or Low Quality**
**Solution:** Add "4K resolution," "sharp focus," "commercial photography quality"

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS

### **Midjourney**
- Use `--style raw` for more photorealistic results
- Use `--s 250` for balanced stylization (lower = more realistic)
- Use `--v 6` for latest version
- `/describe` an existing photo to learn effective prompts

### **DALL-E 3**
- More natural language friendly
- Can handle longer, more descriptive prompts
- Specify "photorealistic" explicitly
- Good at understanding composition instructions

### **Leonardo.ai**
- Use "PhotoReal" model for product photography
- Adjust Guidance Scale (7-8 for realism)
- Use "Photography" preset
- Alchemy feature improves quality

### **Stable Diffusion**
- Use realistic checkpoints: Realistic Vision, DreamShaper
- Lower CFG Scale (6-8) for realism
- Use proper negative prompts
- Higher step count (40-60) for better quality
