*   **The Dangers of a Low-Salt Diet:**
    *   **Increased Sugar Cravings:** A low-salt diet can make your brain's reward system more sensitive, making sugar and other addictive substances more appealing.
    *   **Internal Starvation:** Low salt can lead to insulin resistance, causing your body to store fat and crave carbs, even when you're not truly hungry.
    *   **Reduced Performance:** For athletes or anyone active, salt is crucial for hydration, preventing cramps, and maintaining energy.
