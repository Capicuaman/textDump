# AI Prompt: Generate Sales Emails, Objection Handlers & Sales Collateral

Use this prompt with Claude, ChatGPT, or any LLM to generate sales emails, follow-ups, objection handlers, and sales decks based on BILAN's pain points, benefits, and messaging framework.

---

## **Prompt Template for Sales Email Generation**

```
You are a direct-response copywriter specializing in B2B sales for a health and wellness brand called BILAN (pharmaceutical-grade electrolyte powder).

Your task is to generate [NUMBER] sales email variations based on the following framework:

**BILAN's Pain Points (What prospects struggle with):**
[INSERT FROM painpoints.md - pick the most relevant]

**BILAN's Benefits (How we solve those pain points):**
[INSERT FROM benefits.md - pick the most relevant]

**Sales Messaging Framework:**
- Use the "Problem-Agitate-Partner" structure
- Lead with THEIR problem, not our product
- Use data and specificity (no fluff)
- Always end with clear next steps

**Target Audience:** [SPECIFY: Gym owner, Retailer, Corporate buyer, Influencer, etc.]

**Goal:** [SPECIFY: Get a meeting, overcome price objection, qualify fit, etc.]

**Tone:** Confident, transparent, partner-focused (like we're on the same team solving a problem together)

Please generate [NUMBER] email variations that:
1. Open with a specific, relevant pain point
2. Agitate the cost of not solving it
3. Introduce BILAN as the solution
4. Include proof/data point (case study, stat, testimonial)
5. Close with a specific, easy next step
6. Keep subject line under 50 characters
7. Keep body under 150 words

Format each as:
- Subject Line
- Body
- Why it works (brief explanation)
```

## **Prompt Template for Objection Handler Generation**

```
You are a sales coach helping BILAN's sales team overcome common prospect objections.

**Common Objection:** [INSERT FROM painpoints.md]

**BILAN's Response Framework:**
- Acknowledge the objection with empathy
- Provide transparent, data-backed response
- Pivot to value and partnership
- Offer proof/case studies
- Suggest next steps to remove the objection

**Context:**
- BILAN is a B2B sales company selling to [Gyms/Retailers/Corporate buyers]
- We're positioning BILAN as a partner, not just a vendor
- We offer: [INSERT RELEVANT BENEFITS FROM benefits.md]

Please generate 3 different objection handlers for this objection that:
1. Show you understand their concern (no dismissing)
2. Address the underlying fear transparently
3. Provide 1-2 pieces of proof (stat, case study, guarantee)
4. Reframe as an opportunity, not a problem
5. Include a clear next step

Format each as:
- The Empathetic Acknowledgment
- The Transparent Explanation
- The Proof Point
- The Reframe & Next Step
```

## **Prompt Template for Sales Call Frameworks**

```
You are a sales coach preparing BILAN reps for calls with [PROSPECT TYPE].

**Call Goal:** [Get meeting / Qualify fit / Overcome objection / Close trial / Etc.]

**Prospect Pain Points:** [SELECT FROM painpoints.md]
**BILAN Benefits to Emphasize:** [SELECT FROM benefits.md]
**Messaging Framework:** Problem-Agitate-Partner

Create a sales call framework that includes:

1. **Opening (2 min):**
   - How to set the frame
   - Key question to ask to establish rapport
   - What to listen for

2. **Diagnosis (5 min):**
   - 3-4 diagnostic questions to understand their situation
   - What concerns to listen for
   - How to validate their pain point

3. **Presentation (3 min):**
   - Top 3 proof points for THIS prospect type
   - How BILAN solves their specific problem
   - Why we're different from competitors

4. **Objection Handling (3 min):**
   - Most common objections they'll raise
   - Pre-scripted responses (transparent, data-backed)
   - How to pivot back to value

5. **Closing (2 min):**
   - How to get to "yes" without pressure
   - What next step to suggest (meeting, trial, demo)
   - How to confirm and follow up

Include example dialogue for each section.
```

## **Prompt Template for Sales One-Pagers**

```
You are creating a one-page sales summary for [PROSPECT TYPE] from BILAN.

**Prospect Pain Points:** [SELECT FROM painpoints.md]
**BILAN Benefits:** [SELECT FROM benefits.md]
**Proof Points:** [Case studies, data, testimonials]

Create a one-page summary (suitable for printing or PDF) that includes:

1. **Headline** (10 words max)
   - Problem statement + solution

2. **The Problem** (50 words)
   - What challenge do they face?
   - What's the cost of not solving it?

3. **The BILAN Solution** (75 words)
   - What BILAN does
   - Why we're different
   - Key benefits for THIS prospect type

4. **Proof** (3 bullet points)
   - Case study or stat
   - Proof of quality (certification, testing, etc.)
   - Proof of customer success

5. **Partnership Terms** (Key details)
   - Pricing/Margin (if appropriate)
   - Support we provide
   - Money-back guarantee or trial offer

6. **Next Steps** (Clear CTA)
   - What happens if they say yes
   - How to get started
   - Who to contact

Use clear formatting, minimal text, strong visuals/icons, and a call-to-action at the bottom.
```

## **Prompt Template for Case Study Generation**

```
You are writing a case study for BILAN's sales team featuring [PARTNER NAME/TYPE].

**Partner Details:**
- Type: [Gym/Retail/Corporate]
- Challenge before BILAN: [From painpoints.md]
- Solution provided by BILAN: [From benefits.md]
- Results achieved: [Specific numbers if possible]

Create a case study that:

1. **Opens with a specific problem** they faced
   - Use exact quotes if possible
   - Show quantified pain (e.g., "losing $X in margin")

2. **Explains how they found BILAN**
   - Who reached out and how
   - Initial concerns/objections

3. **Details the solution**
   - What BILAN provided
   - How implementation went
   - What we supported them with

4. **Quantifies the results**
   - Sales growth (%)
   - Margin improvement
   - Customer satisfaction
   - Reorder rates
   - Revenue impact

5. **Includes a quote from decision maker**
   - Testimonial about the partnership
   - What they value most
   - How it changed their business

6. **Ends with next steps for reader**
   - Clear CTA
   - How to replicate the success

Format: 400-500 words, storytelling style, heavy on numbers.
```

## **How to Use These Prompts**

1. **Pick the right prompt** based on what you need (email, objection handler, call script, etc.)
2. **Fill in the brackets** with:
   - Specific pain points from `painpoints.md`
   - Relevant benefits from `benefits.md`
   - Proof points (case studies, data, testimonials) you have on hand
3. **Paste into Claude, ChatGPT, or preferred LLM**
4. **Regenerate and refine** as needed (use follow-ups like "Make this more direct" or "Add more specificity")
5. **Save outputs** to the `output/` folder for reuse across the sales team

---

## **Pro Tips for Better Outputs**

✅ **Be specific about the prospect type** - "Gym owner in premium fitness" vs. just "Gym owner"  
✅ **Include 1-2 proof points** from real BILAN partnerships when available  
✅ **Specify the goal of the email/call** - Clarity leads to better copy  
✅ **Add constraints** - "Keep under 100 words," "Use active voice," "No fluff"  
✅ **Request variations** - "Give me 3 different approaches: aggressive, educational, relationship-focused"  
✅ **Ask for rankings** - "Rank these by conversion potential and explain why"

## **Example: Filled-In Prompt (Retail Partner Email)**

```
You are a direct-response copywriter specializing in B2B sales for BILAN (pharmaceutical-grade electrolyte powder).

Your task is to generate 3 sales email variations for reaching out to premium retailers about stocking BILAN.

**Pain Points (What retailers struggle with):**
- Finding high-margin products that actually sell
- Dead inventory from products customers don't ask for
- Limited vendor support (vendors disappear post-contract)

**Benefits (How BILAN solves this):**
- Clear 40% wholesale margin + reorder incentives
- Transparent, data-backed demand generation (BILAN funds ads)
- Dedicated account manager + ongoing training

**Target Audience:** Retail wellness shop owner in premium segment
**Goal:** Get a 15-minute call to discuss partnership
**Tone:** Partner-focused, data-driven, confident

Please generate 3 email variations that open with the pain point, agitate the cost of not solving it, introduce BILAN, include a proof point, and end with a specific next step. Keep subject lines under 50 characters and body under 150 words.
```
