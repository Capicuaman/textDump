# How to Sign Up as a Seller on Mercado Libre

This document outlines the steps to create a seller account on Mercado Libre.

## 1. Determine your seller type

Decide if you will register as an individual or a company. This will dictate the information and digital files you'll need to provide.

## 2. Create your Mercado Libre account

*   Go to the Mercado Libre website for the country where you want to sell.
*   Look for the "create account" option and complete the registration form. You'll need to provide your company information if registering as a business.
*   After signing up, you'll receive an email to verify your account. Follow the link in the email and log in to your new account.

## 3. Complete additional information

Fill in any further required details and confirm them.

## 4. Set selling preferences

You may be directed to a "Selling Preferences" screen. Click on "Add a marketplace" and select the marketplace where you wish to operate.

## 5. List your products

*   Log in to your account and select the "sell" option.
*   Choose the type of product you are going to publish (e.g., product, vehicle, real estate, or services).
*   Write a clear title for your listing, including the product, brand, model, and main features.
*   Select the appropriate main category and then a specific subcategory for your product.
*   Complete all product information, including stock, universal product code, price, publication type, key characteristics for the technical sheet, shipping method, warranty information, invoicing, and a detailed product description.
*   Upload professional, high-resolution photos of your product from different angles. The first photo should have a white background.
*   Click "Publish" to make your product available for sale.
