## üí° CONVERSION BOOSTERS - Increase Success Rate by 30%

### Before Initial Contact

**1. Research the Decision-Maker** (15 min per lead)
- Check their Facebook profile for business interests
- Find at least 2-3 facts about their business
- Identify their specific pain point
- Example: "I noticed B-Fitness already sells protein shakes - electrolytes would complement that perfectly"

**2. Prepare Specific Value Statement**
Instead of: "We sell electrolytes"
Say: "We provide an electrolyte solution that gyms with supplement programs have found increases member satisfaction 30-40% and creates a new revenue stream"

**3. Have Samples Ready**
- Show, don't tell - people buy what they can taste
- Offer trial for staff first (they become brand ambassadors)
- Premium packaging matters more in health stores/spas

### During Initial Contact

**4. Lead with Their Pain Point, Not Your Product**
- Gym: "How are members responding to your current supplement selection?"
- Pharmacy: "What electrolyte products are customers asking for?"
- Spa: "What do clients request post-treatment for recovery?"

**5. Social Proof**
- "We've already successfully partnered with [similar business type in their city]"
- Show reviews/testimonials from similar businesses
- Mention if any competitor is already stocking in M√©rida

**6. Test Close Early**
- "Would you be interested in trying one case with your staff first?"
- Make first order EASY (small quantity, flexible terms)
- Remove risk by offering buyback guarantee on first order
