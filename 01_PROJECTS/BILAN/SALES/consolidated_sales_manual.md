# Consolidated Sales Manual

## Part 1: Advertising

### Objective
To accelerate customer acquisition and drive targeted traffic to BILAN's sales channels through paid advertising campaigns.

### Key Strategies & Actionable Steps

#### 1. Social Media Advertising
Reaching potential customers on the platforms where they spend their time.

*   **Strategy:** Run highly targeted ad campaigns on Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Define and create detailed target audience personas (e.g., "The Marathon Runner," "The Yoga Enthusiast," "The Busy Professional").
    *   **Actionable Step:** A/B test different ad creatives (images vs. videos), copy, and calls-to-action to identify what resonates most with each audience segment.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Implement retargeting campaigns to re-engage users who have visited the BILAN website but have not made a purchase.

#### 2. Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
Capturing high-intent customers who are actively searching for solutions.

*   **Strategy:** Bid on relevant keywords on Google Ads to appear in front of users searching for electrolyte and hydration products.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Create a comprehensive list of target keywords, including both broad terms ("electrolyte powder") and long-tail keywords ("best electrolyte drink for runners").
    *   **Actionable Step:** Design and optimize dedicated landing pages for ad campaigns to ensure a seamless user experience and maximize conversions.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Monitor campaign performance closely, adjusting bids and keywords to optimize for return on ad spend (ROAS).

#### 3. Influencer Marketing
Leveraging the trust and reach of established voices in the health and wellness community.

*   **Strategy:** Collaborate with paid influencers to promote BILAN to their engaged audiences.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Identify and vet influencers whose audience and values align with the BILAN brand.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Negotiate clear deliverables, including the number of posts, stories, and the type of content to be created.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Track the performance of influencer campaigns using unique discount codes or affiliate links.

#### 4. Podcast Advertising
Reaching a captive audience through sponsorships of relevant podcasts.

*   **Strategy:** Sponsor health, fitness, and wellness podcasts that are popular with our target demographic.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Research and identify podcasts that have a high concentration of listeners in our target audience.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Create compelling and authentic ad scripts that can be read by the podcast host.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Use unique URLs or discount codes to track the effectiveness of each podcast sponsorship.

## Part 2: One-on-One Outreach

### Objective
To build genuine relationships and drive brand awareness for BILAN through direct, personal interaction. This manual outlines the strategies and actionable steps for effective one-on-one outreach.

### Key Strategies & Actionable Steps

#### 1. In-Person Networking & Events
Engaging with potential customers and partners in environments where health and wellness are a focus.

*   **Strategy:** Attend industry-relevant events, such as fitness expos, wellness conferences, and local marathons.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Identify and create a calendar of at least 5 key local and regional events to attend per quarter.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Prepare an "event kit" including BILAN samples, business cards, and informational flyers.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Set a goal for each event, such as "make 20 new contacts" or "secure one partnership."

*   **Strategy:** Partner with local businesses for in-store demos and workshops.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Create a target list of 20 local gyms, yoga studios, and health food stores for potential partnerships.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Develop a standardized demo script and setup that can be easily deployed.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Follow up with all leads and contacts within 48 hours of the event.

#### 2. Direct Digital Engagement
Using online platforms to create personal connections with individuals.

*   **Strategy:** Engage directly with potential customers and micro-influencers on social media (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook).
    *   **Actionable Step:** Identify 50 potential influencers and engaged users to interact with weekly.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Personalize all outreach messages. Reference their recent posts or interests. Avoid generic, copy-pasted templates.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Participate in relevant conversations in comments sections and forums.

#### 3. Community Involvement
Building brand presence and goodwill by being active in the local community.

*   **Strategy:** Sponsor or participate in local sports teams, clubs, or wellness events.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Research and identify local community events or teams that align with the BILAN brand.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Offer BILAN products as prizes or refreshments at events.
    *   **Actionable Step:** Actively participate in the events, don't just provide sponsorship. Have a presence and engage with attendees.
