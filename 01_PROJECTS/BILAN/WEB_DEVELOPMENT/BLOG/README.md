# WEB_DEVELOPMENT/BLOG - Blog Strategy and Planning

This directory contains **blog content strategy, planning documents, and SEO roadmaps**.

## Purpose

Strategic planning and content architecture for the BILAN blog, including SEO content clusters, editorial calendars, and blog post ideas.

## Contents

- `blog_pages.md` - Comprehensive blog content strategy
  - Content vs lead-gen strategy
  - SEO pillar content planning
  - Differentiation content ideas
  - Use-case content mapping
  - Blog page structure and funnel design
- `blogposts.md` - Blog post ideas and outlines

## Strategic Focus Areas

### Content Clusters
1. **Dehydration Problems** (High Intent)
   - Symptoms, causes, solutions
2. **Electrolytes Education** (Awareness)
   - What they are, why they matter
3. **Use-Case Content** (Conversion)
   - Exercise, heat, recovery, fasting

### Blog Page Types
- **SEO Content Hub** (`/blog` or `/aprende`)
- **Educational Content** (Top of funnel)
- **Comparison Pages** (Buyer intent)
- **Trust Pages** (Ingredients, FAQ, mission)
