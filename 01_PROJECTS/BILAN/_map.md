# Project BILAN Directory Map

This document outlines the new, organized structure of the BILAN project directory.

- **/business/**: Contains strategic business documents, including pricing, marketing plans, and discount structures.
- **/competitiveAnalysis/**: Holds research and analysis on competing brands, primarily LMNT.
- **/marketing/**: A collection of all marketing materials, including slogans, sticker designs, and specific campaigns.
- **/packaging/**: Design templates for product packaging.
- **/product/**: Contains all scientific and informational documents about the BILAN product itself.
- **/RAG/**: Houses the data and server for the Retrieval-Augmented Generation application.
- **/RUNNING/**: Specific materials related to electrolytes for runners.
- **/SALES/**: The complete sales playbook, broken down by section.
- **/web/**: Contains materials for BILAN's online presence, including the blog and SEO strategies.
- **/metaPrompts/**: Prompts for internal AI/LLM usage.
