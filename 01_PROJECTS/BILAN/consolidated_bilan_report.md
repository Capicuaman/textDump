# Bilan Consolidated Report

## Overview
Bilan is a company specializing in the production and sales of electrolyte salt. The brand focuses on providing a clean and natural alternative for athletes and fitness enthusiasts looking to maintain optimal hydration during their activities.

## Products
- **Electrolyte Mix**: Formulated to replace sodium, potassium, and magnesium lost through sweat.
  - **Dosage**: Recommended dosage is 3g to be dissolved in 300ml of water.
  - **Slogan**: Promotes "clean functional hydration," highlighting its natural, additive-free formulation.
  - **Available Sizes**: 200g and 1kg packs of electrolyte mix.

## Marketing and Sales
- **Sales Strategy**:
  - Direct-to-consumer (DTC) approach via subscriptions through the website.
  - Wholesale arrangements with gyms and clinics.
  - B2B partnerships targeting sports teams and corporate wellness programs.
- **Target Audience**:
  - **Primary**: Athletes and fitness enthusiasts.
  - **Secondary**: Corporate wellness programs and medical clinics.
