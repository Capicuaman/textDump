## Strategy at a Glance

### Target Platforms (First 3 Months):
1. **Instagram** (Primary - 60% effort)
2. **TikTok** (Secondary - 30% effort)
3. **WhatsApp Status** (Tertiary - 10% effort)

### Content Distribution:
- 40% Education (science, tips, myths)
- 25% Lifestyle (routines, use cases)
- 20% Community (testimonials, UGC)
- 10% Product (features, launches)
- 5% Engagement (entertainment, trends)

### Posting Frequency:
- **Instagram Feed:** 4-5x per week
- **Instagram Reels:** 3-4x per week
- **Instagram Stories:** Daily (5-10 per day)
- **TikTok:** 4-5x per week
- **WhatsApp Status:** 2-3x per day

### Success Metrics (Month 1 Goals):
- 500-1,000 Instagram followers
- 3-5% engagement rate
- 10-20 website clicks per week
- 5-10 DM inquiries per week

---

## Core Principles

1. **Consistency Over Perfection** - Post regularly even if content isn't "perfect"
2. **Educate First, Sell Second** - Provide value before asking for purchases
3. **Engage Authentically** - Build real relationships, not just follower counts
4. **Data-Driven Decisions** - Track metrics and adjust based on performance
5. **Long-Term Mindset** - Organic growth takes 90-120 days to gain momentum

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "I don't know what to post today"
**Solution:** Refer to the 50 content ideas in **content_batching_template.md** or the 20 quick-win ideas in **kickstart_strategy.md**

### "My engagement rate is low"
**Solution:**
1. Check if you're using the right hashtags (quick_reference_guide.md)
2. Increase your daily engagement from 30 min to 45 min
3. Post at optimal times (9-11 AM or 7-9 PM)
4. Focus more on education content (highest engagement pillar)

### "I'm running out of time"
**Solution:** Use the batching method from **content_batching_template.md** to create 2 weeks of content in one 3-4 hour session

### "I don't know if my content is working"
**Solution:** Track the 7 metrics listed in **quick_reference_guide.md** and review weekly. Double down on what's working, eliminate what's not.

### "I'm not getting any followers"
**Solution:**
1. Ensure you're posting consistently (4-5x per week minimum)
2. Use all 30 hashtags on Instagram posts
3. Increase engagement with other accounts (target: 20-30 per day)
4. Make sure your content is providing value, not just promotional

## Monthly Review Process

**Every 30 days, review:**

1. **Growth:** Did we hit follower count goals?
2. **Engagement:** Is engagement rate 3-5% or higher?
3. **Content:** What were the top 3 performing posts? Why did they work?
4. **Audience:** What questions are people asking in comments/DMs?
5. **Conversion:** Are we getting website clicks and inquiries?
6. **Strategy:** What should we continue, stop, or start doing?

Update your strategy based on these insights and document learnings.
