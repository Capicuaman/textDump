## TIKTOK-SPECIFIC BEST PRACTICES

### Filming Tips:
1. **Vertical format only** (9:16 aspect ratio)
2. **Film in natural light** when possible
3. **Use phone's native camera** then edit in CapCut
4. **Keep camera steady** or use intentional movement
5. **Hook in first 1-2 seconds** is CRITICAL

### Editing Tips:
1. **Use CapCut** (free, TikTok-optimized)
2. **Add trending sounds** from TikTok's library
3. **Text overlays** should be large, easy to read
4. **Captions** on by default - design for sound-off viewing
5. **Quick cuts** every 2-3 seconds maintain attention
6. **Transitions** on beat drops increase engagement

### Audio Strategy:
1. **Check daily trending sounds** in FYP
2. **Use trending audio** within 3-5 days of peak
3. **Original audio** works for educational content
4. **Voiceover** over trending instrumental = best of both
5. **Clear audio quality** - no background noise

### Text Overlay Guidelines:
1. **Large, bold fonts**
2. **High contrast** (white text on dark bg, etc.)
3. **Avoid TikTok's auto-caption area** (bottom center)
4. **Emoji usage** enhances readability
5. **Brief text** - 3-7 words max per overlay

## TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### Problem: Video stuck at 200-300 views
**Solution:**
- Check if audio is copyrighted/muted
- Ensure video is 9:16 vertical format
- Hook might be weak - remake with stronger hook
- Delete and repost at different time

### Problem: Low engagement (few comments/likes)
**Solution:**
- Add clear CTA in caption asking question
- Respond to every comment to boost engagement
- Content may be too promotional - add more value
- Try educational angle instead of sales angle

### Problem: High views but low conversions
**Solution:**
- Add clearer CTA in video
- Pin comment with direct link
- Create urgency (limited discount, timer)
- Follow up with "how to order" tutorial video

### Problem: Comments asking same questions
**Solution:**
- Create FAQ video
- Pin comprehensive comment with answers
- Add info to bio
- Create "Part 2" video addressing questions
