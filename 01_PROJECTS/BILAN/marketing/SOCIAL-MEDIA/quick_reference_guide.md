## 30-MINUTE DAILY ENGAGEMENT

**Morning (10 min):**
- Respond to all comments on posts (past 24 hours)
- Reply to all DMs (personalized responses)
- Check and acknowledge tagged posts/stories

**Midday (10 min):**
- Engage with 20-30 niche accounts (thoughtful 4+ word comments)
- Follow aligned accounts (fitness, health, wellness)
- Participate in hashtag conversations

**Evening (10 min):**
- Post daily stories
- Engage with active followers' content
- Run interactive story features (polls, questions)

## WEEKLY METRICS TO TRACK

Record every Monday morning in spreadsheet:

1. Total followers (Instagram + TikTok)
2. Follower growth percentage
3. Average engagement rate (total engagement / total followers x 100)
4. Top performing post (link + engagement rate)
5. Profile visits
6. Link clicks
7. DM inquiries
8. Notable observations/patterns

**Benchmark Goals (Month 1):**
- 500-1,000 Instagram followers
- 3-5% engagement rate
- 10-20 website clicks/week
- 5-10 DM inquiries/week

## RED FLAGS TO WATCH

If you see these, pivot immediately:
- Less than 1% engagement rate after 30 days
- High unfollow rate (more unfollows than follows)
- Low story completion rates (under 50%)
- No DM inquiries or website clicks

**Solution:** Review top-performing content, double down on what works, eliminate what doesn't.

---

## SUCCESS MANTRAS

1. Consistency beats perfection
2. Educate first, sell second
3. Engage authentically, not transactionally
4. Track data, make informed decisions
5. Give it 90 days before judging results
