# Man Drinking - Video Prompt

## Scene Description

A fit man in his 30s, wearing athletic clothing (gray compression shirt), stands in a modern, minimalist kitchen with bright natural morning light. He's just finished a workout - there's a subtle sheen of perspiration on his face and arms, suggesting physical exertion. He reaches for a sports bottle filled with clear electrolyte solution sitting on a white marble countertop.

The man picks up the bottle with a sense of purpose and anticipation. As he brings it to his lips, the camera captures his expression - a mix of determination and relief. He takes a long, satisfying drink, tilting the bottle at 45 degrees. His eyes close briefly in the moment of refreshment, and a subtle look of contentment crosses his face.

The clear electrolyte solution is visible through the transparent sports bottle. As he drinks, small bubbles rise in the liquid. When he lowers the bottle, there's a sense of immediate revitalization - his shoulders relax slightly, he exhales with satisfaction, and his expression shows renewed energy.

The environment remains clean and minimalist throughout - white marble counters, soft natural lighting from a window, neutral tones dominating the space.

- **Emotion:** Relief, satisfaction, revitalization
- **Setting:** Modern minimalist kitchen, morning light
