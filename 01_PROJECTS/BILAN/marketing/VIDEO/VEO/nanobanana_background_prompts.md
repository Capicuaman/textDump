## Usage Guidelines

### Technical Specifications
- All prompts are formatted for 16:9 aspect ratio
- Photorealistic style recommended for brand consistency
- "No people" specification keeps focus on environments
- Natural lighting preferred for healthy, clean aesthetic

### Brand Alignment
- Clean, minimalist aesthetics reflect BILAN's pure ingredient philosophy
- Bright, natural lighting emphasizes health and vitality
- Professional environments support scientific credibility
- Active lifestyle backgrounds resonate with target demographics

### Implementation Notes
- Use these backgrounds with existing BILAN product footage
- Can be combined with VEO prompts for seamless video generation
- Maintain consistent lighting and color grading across campaigns
- Consider seasonal variations (e.g., winter indoor scenes, summer outdoor activities)
