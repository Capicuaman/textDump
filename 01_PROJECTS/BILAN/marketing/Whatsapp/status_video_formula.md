**WhatsApp Status Video Formula**

1.  **Hook (1-3 seconds):** Grab attention immediately.
    *   Ask a provocative question.
    *   Show a surprising visual.
    *   State a bold claim.
2.  **Problem/Value (3-5 seconds):** Quickly present the problem or the value proposition.
    *   "Tired of afternoon slumps?"
    *   "Unlock your full potential."
3.  **Solution (5-10 seconds):** Introduce BILAN as the solution.
    *   Show the product being mixed and enjoyed.
    *   Quickly list 1-2 key benefits.
4.  **Call to Action (2-3 seconds):** Tell them what to do next.
    *   "Swipe up to learn more!"
    *   "DM us for a discount!"
    *   "Visit our website!"
