# BILAN Mexican States Regional Campaign

## Campaign Overview

The **BILAN Mexican States Campaign** is a comprehensive regional marketing initiative featuring 32 unique subtly animated vertical videos (9:16, 5-8 seconds each) - one per Mexican state. Each video showcases a BILAN electrolyte product in the foreground with iconic state-specific natural landmarks in the background, designed to create emotional connections with regional audiences through cultural authenticity and regional pride.

**Campaign ID:** `mexican_states_2025`
**Duration:** 7 weeks production + ongoing distribution
**Total Assets:** 32 VEO-generated videos
**Target Platforms:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Stories
**Primary Goal:** Regional sales targeting through culturally resonant content

## Technical Specifications

### Video Format
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 pixels
- **Duration:** 5-8 seconds
- **Frame Rate:** 24-30fps (120fps for slow motion elements)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **File Size:** 10-25 MB per video

### Visual Standards
- **Product Position:** 20-30% of lower-middle frame
- **Negative Space:** 30-35% at top for text overlays
- **Depth of Field:** Shallow (f/2.8) - product sharp, background soft-focus
- **Lighting:** Natural (5000K-5500K color temperature)
- **Style:** Photorealistic, clean aesthetic, natural

### Animation Style
- **Intensity:** 3-4 on scale of 1-10 (subtle, barely noticeable)
- **Focus:** Product remains sharp and static; background has gentle motion
- **Types:** Water ripples, wind movement, mist drifting, heat shimmer
- **Purpose:** Enhance scene without distracting from product

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why start with Quintana Roo?**
A: Quintana Roo's cenote imagery is highly distinctive and visually testable. It validates the template before scaling to all 32 states.

**Q: How long does it take to customize each prompt?**
A: 30-45 minutes per state if you have state research handy and understand the template structure.

**Q: How many videos can I generate per day?**
A: Realistic goal is 2-3 complete videos per day (customization + generation + QC).

**Q: What if VEO doesn't generate a good video on first try?**
A: Budget allows for 2-3 attempts per state. Follow iteration guidelines in production guide.

**Q: Do all 32 videos need to look identical?**
A: They should be visually cohesive (same product positioning, lighting style, brand aesthetic) while celebrating each state's unique landscape.

**Q: Can I change the master template?**
A: Yes, after generating the first 3 test videos, adjust the template based on learnings. Document changes.

**Q: What if a state landmark doesn't work well?**
A: State research document includes backup landmark options. Consult with local stakeholders for validation.

**Q: How do I know if my video passes quality control?**
A: Use the comprehensive quality checklist in production guide. Score should be 7+/10.
