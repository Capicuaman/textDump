# BILAN Mexican States Campaign - Strategic Plan

## Executive Summary

The Mexican States Regional Campaign is a comprehensive video content initiative designed to drive regional sales through culturally authentic, geo-targeted social media content. By creating 32 unique subtly animated videos (one per Mexican state) featuring BILAN products with iconic state-specific natural landmarks, we establish emotional connections with regional audiences while maintaining brand consistency.

**Campaign Duration:** 7 weeks (production) + ongoing distribution
**Total Assets:** 32 VEO-generated vertical videos (9:16, 5-8 seconds each)
**Primary Platforms:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Stories
**Target Audience:** Health-conscious consumers, active lifestyle individuals (25-45), regional pride-driven purchasers

---

## Strategic Rationale

### Why Regional Targeting?

1. **Cultural Connection:** Mexicans have strong regional identity and pride. Seeing their state's landmarks creates immediate emotional resonance.

2. **Geo-Targeting Efficiency:** Social media platforms allow precise geographic targeting, ensuring each video reaches its intended audience with minimal waste.

3. **Word-of-Mouth Amplification:** Regional pride drives sharing. When someone from Jalisco sees the Oblatos Canyon video, they're likely to share it with friends/family ("¡Mira, es Jalisco!").

4. **Market Penetration:** Mexico's 32 states have distinct cultures, climates, and lifestyles. A one-size-fits-all approach misses regional nuances.

5. **E-Commerce Optimization:** Regional content can be paired with state-specific product listings on MercadoLibre and other platforms, driving localized conversions.

### Why Subtle Animation vs. Static Images?

1. **Algorithm Favorability:** Social media algorithms prioritize video content over static images (2-3x higher reach).

2. **Stopping Power:** Even subtle motion (water ripples, leaves swaying) increases scroll-stopping power by 40% vs. static content.

3. **Loop-ability:** 5-8 second videos designed for seamless looping keep viewers engaged longer, boosting engagement metrics.

4. **Emotional Impact:** Motion creates life and dynamism while maintaining focus on the product. Static images feel less engaging.

5. **Versatility:** Video content performs across multiple platforms (Reels, TikTok, Shorts, Stories) without reformatting.

## Tiered Rollout Strategy

### Tier 1: High-Priority States (10 states) - Weeks 1-3

**States:** Ciudad de México, Estado de México, Jalisco, Veracruz, Puebla, Guanajuato, Nuevo León, Chiapas, Quintana Roo, Yucatán

**Why Prioritize:**
- Combined population: >55 million (>40% of Mexico)
- Highest e-commerce penetration and digital adoption
- Largest BILAN target demographic (urban, health-conscious, 25-45)
- Fastest path to ROI validation

**Distribution Strategy:**
- Launch all 10 videos simultaneously across platforms
- Heavy geo-targeting on Instagram and TikTok
- Paid amplification budget: 60% of total campaign budget
- Partner with 3-5 local influencers per state

**Success Metrics (Tier 1):**
- 2M+ combined views across all platforms
- >4.5% engagement rate
- 10K+ website clicks with regional attribution
- 500+ e-commerce conversions

## Geo-Targeting & Regional Activation

### Social Media Geo-Targeting

**Instagram:**
- State-level location targeting
- Interest targeting: Fitness, wellness, healthy living, outdoor activities
- Lookalike audiences from existing customers in each state
- Exclude users outside target state for precision

**TikTok:**
- State-level location targeting (less precise than Instagram)
- Leverage regional hashtags for organic discovery
- Spark Ads to amplify high-performing organic content

**Facebook (Supporting):**
- Detailed location targeting by state and major cities
- Interest layering: Health & wellness, fitness, natural products
- Retargeting website visitors by state

### Regional Influencer Partnerships

**Tier 1 States (3-5 influencers per state):**
- Micro-influencers (10K-50K followers) with strong local following
- Fitness trainers, yoga instructors, nutritionists, outdoor adventurers
- Authentic connection to state and healthy lifestyle
- Content collaboration: Influencer creates their own version using BILAN + state landmark

**Tier 2 & 3 States (1-3 influencers per state):**
- Nano-influencers (5K-15K) with highly engaged local communities
- Focus on authentic brand advocates over large reach
- Gifting strategy with content rights

**Influencer Activation:**
1. **Seeding Phase:** Send product + campaign video to influencers 2 weeks before public launch
2. **Content Brief:** Encourage influencers to create their own state-specific content
3. **Hashtag Campaign:** #BILANenMiEstado (BILAN in My State) for UGC aggregation
4. **Ongoing Engagement:** Repost influencer content to BILAN official channels

## Risk Management & Mitigation

### Potential Risks

1. **VEO Generation Inconsistency**
   - **Risk:** Videos don't match quality across states
   - **Mitigation:** Establish visual style guide from first 3 videos; strict QC checklist before distribution
   - **Contingency:** Budget for re-generation if quality doesn't meet standards

2. **Landmark Non-Recognition**
   - **Risk:** State residents don't recognize or connect with chosen landmarks
   - **Mitigation:** Validate landmark selections with local stakeholders/influencers before generation
   - **Contingency:** Maintain list of backup landmarks per state for re-shoots if needed

3. **Low Engagement in Certain States**
   - **Risk:** Some states underperform engagement benchmarks
   - **Mitigation:** Heavier influencer activation and paid amplification in underperforming states
   - **Contingency:** Shift budget from high-performing to low-performing states dynamically

4. **Regional Targeting Ineffective**
   - **Risk:** Videos don't reach target state audiences effectively
   - **Mitigation:** Use multiple targeting layers (location + interests + lookalikes)
   - **Contingency:** Partner with local influencers for direct audience access

5. **Timeline Delays**
   - **Risk:** VEO generation or approval processes extend beyond 7 weeks
   - **Mitigation:** Build 1-week buffer into timeline; prioritize Tier 1 for immediate impact
   - **Contingency:** Tier 3 can be delayed without impacting core campaign goals

6. **Budget Overruns**
   - **Risk:** VEO generation costs or paid amplification exceed budget
   - **Mitigation:** Set hard caps on VEO attempts per state (max 3); monitor paid ad CPM daily
   - **Contingency:** Reduce Tier 3 paid amplification to organic-only if budget constrained
