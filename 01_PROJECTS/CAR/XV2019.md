## üìã Specifications Overview

### Engine & Performance
- **Engine Type**: 155cc single-cylinder, 4-stroke, liquid-cooled
- **Horsepower**: 19.5 hp @ 10,500 rpm
- **Torque**: 10.3 lb-ft @ 7,500 rpm
- **Transmission**: 5-speed manual
- **Fuel System**: PGM-FI (Programmed Fuel Injection)

### Dimensions & Weight
- **Overall Length**: 1,915 mm (75.4 in)
- **Overall Width**: 745 mm (29.3 in)
- **Overall Height**: 1,115 mm (43.9 in)
- **Seat Height**: 785 mm (30.9 in)
- **Wheelbase**: 1,300 mm (51.2 in)
- **Curb Weight**: 137 kg (302 lbs)

### Chassis & Suspension
- **Front Suspension**: 31mm telescopic fork
- **Rear Suspension**: Monocross with single shock, 125mm travel
- **Front Brake**: Single 266mm disc with 2-piston caliper
- **Rear Brake**: Single 220mm disc with single-piston caliper
- **Front Tire**: 100/80-17M/C 52P
- **Rear Tire**: 140/70-17M/C 66P

## üèçÔ∏è Riding Tips

### For Beginners
- **Smooth Throttle Control**: The single-cylinder engine responds well to smooth inputs
- **Gear Shifting**: Use the clutch for smooth gear changes
- **Braking Practice**: Progressive braking with both front and rear brakes
- **Cornering**: Look through the corner and maintain steady throttle

### Urban Riding
- **Filter Pore**: Use the air filter in heavy traffic
- **Parking**: Lock the steering and use the center stand on level ground
- **Fuel Economy**: Expect 45-55 mpg (21-26 km/l) in mixed riding

### Highway Riding
- **Wind Protection**: Consider aftermarket windscreen for speeds above 60 mph
- **Riding Position**: Tuck in at higher speeds to reduce fatigue
- **Engine RPM**: Keep engine between 6,000-8,000 rpm for optimal efficiency

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Hard Starting**: Check battery voltage and fuel quality
- **Poor Performance**: Clean air filter, check spark plug
- **Chain Noise**: Adjust chain tension and lubricate
- **Brake Squeal**: Clean brake calipers and check pad wear

### Warning Lights
- **Check Engine Light**: Usually indicates sensor or emissions issue
- **Oil Pressure Light**: Stop immediately and check oil level
- **Temperature Warning**: Allow engine to cool, check coolant level
